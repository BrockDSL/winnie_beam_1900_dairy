August 17
In the afternnon Dr
Abbott drove down for
Mamma & I. Mrs
Klots was going, but sent
wprd. We drove out
Martindale way, past
Overholts down Pollywog
road through Port
and home. Pappa
& Mamma went to
Dr Klot's at night Dr
Abbott & Mrs Tulley were
there. Tried to get butter
at Lee's & Dummin's, but failed.


August 18
