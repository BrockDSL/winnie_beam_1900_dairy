July 10
Was over to Mc.Laren's
after beans. Then came
home and went to 
Greenwoods for Phoebe
and to library. Got 
"The Throne of David" By 
Ingraham. This afternoon 
I dropped up and went
over to Allies. But she was 
at Sunday SChool picnic. Then 
I went to see Ressie Read[?]
Had two Sunday School 
excursions from Grounds in the park
to-day. 
Papa & mamma drove to the cemetary.

July 11
Was over to Carman's
in the Afternoon to
ask Ella to go to decew
falls with us this 
afternoon. But when 
afternoon came it looked
to rainy that we couldn't 
go. But we went for a 
drive around the streets. 
Had a thunderstorm and
a very high winds. From 
Triumph had their pricnic 
at Mc.Callas Grove and St. 
Paul & Niagara St. Had theirs[?]
to Grimsby.
