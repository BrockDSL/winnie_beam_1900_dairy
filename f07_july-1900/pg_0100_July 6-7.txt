July 6
Very warm it has been
the worst day this year.
All the foreigners are surprised 
to have been killed in
pekin[?]. I am kniting
a wash cloth. Did not
stir much. We are to
have a memorial service 
in our church Sunday 
for P. Livin [?] who died in
Smith Africa July 1. He was
There first contingent[?]
Mrs. MArman & Ella were 
over to see the rambler[?]

July 7
went to market after
peas, and to Eckphardt's [?]
after my music. Got Godaids
"Valse charmatigue'[?] but not
the other. Was all Wilsons
had a large excursion in 
the park for Toronto. They 
Dominion Radiator Co. They 
had a [?]
park nine V themselves. Had
the grenadier Baud. Papa & 
mamma went frog shooting. 
The buffalo [?] church 
played a match with us
this afternoon.
