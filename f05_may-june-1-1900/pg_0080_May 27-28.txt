May 27
Papa and I started at 10:30 for
Queenston. We got there i
time for dinner Had a blue picknik. Had it at
the monument House. There 
Papa and I went down to the railway, but the ticket agent
was not there and we went after
him. Went as far as chippawa
then when we came back we 
got off at Niagara Falls and went over
to American[?] side throug hthe park. 
Then when we came back we stopped
at Brock's monument and saw the
forts. Had a fish supper and started home
10 min after seven got home at nine. 
Met Mr. F. Taylor & wife and Mr. Auem[?] and his wife.

May 28
Mamma & I drove up to 
Cannoll's[?] Took Mrs. Carroll some 
Lilacs. She is very
Miserable Lillie has gone to
New York and Mrs. Kilnir[?]
is all alone. We drove up 
to office. I took my 
hopium Masurkas & vaises to 
have them ground together. 
Mamma & I were quite 
west when we got home as 
it rained hard for a litte
while.
