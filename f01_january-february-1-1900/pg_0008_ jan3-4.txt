<h2><span>January</span><span>&nbsp;</span><span>Wednesday 3</span><span>(3-362)</span><span>1900</span></h2>

<p><a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/communitymembers#TrustySusan" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Mrs Trusty</a> was here washing<br />
<a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/locations#CollegiateInstitute" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">School</a> started to-day, but I<br />

Mamma went to the <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/locations#StThomas" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">church</a><br />
and then to <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/communitymembers#ChatfieldMrs" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Mrs Chatfields</a><br />
Took her the church books.<br />

had a service of praise this<br />
evening. <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/communitymembers#Chapman" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Miss V Chapman</a> is<br />
organist &amp; Mrs Read-Muir sang<br />
<a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/communitymembers/#ArmstrongGeorge" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Mrs George Armstrong</a> has a<br />
daughter was born on New<br />

<h2><span>&nbsp;</span><span>&nbsp;</span><span>Thursday 4</span><span>(4-361)</span><span>&nbsp;</span></h2>

<p>Went over to Carman&#39;s to have<br />
<a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/communitymembers#CarmanElla" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Ella</a> go with me to <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/locations#Dunn" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Dunn&#39;s<br />
greenhouse</a>. We went about half<br />
past three. I brought a primrose<br />
<a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/communitymembers#ChaplinMiss" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Miss Chaplin</a> was in there.<br />
Mamma went to <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/communitymembers#KlotzMarie" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Mrs Klotz</a><br />
at home <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/communitymembers#DwyerBeatrice" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Beatrice</a> helped.<br />
Papa &amp; I went to Sunday School<br />
entertainment at night. Had<br />
a very long programme. There<br />
was a dialogue called <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/literature#AuntKitty" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">&quot;Aunt Ketty&#39;s [sic]<br />
shopping</a> which was good, and<br />
a shaving scene. <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/communitymembers#Perry" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Mr Perry</a> gave<br />
a tableful of prizes away. Beatrice<br />
came home with us. They had<br />
a drill of girls &amp; they sang<br />
<a>&quot;Soldiers of the Queen&quot;</a></p>
