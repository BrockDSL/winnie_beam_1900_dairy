<h2><span>January</span><span>&nbsp;</span><span>Thursday 11</span><span>(11-354)</span><span>1900</span></h2>

<p>Did not go to <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/locations/#CollegiateInstitute" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">school</a> in the <br />afternoon. The &quot;Musician&quot; for  <br />January came to-night. I have  <br />to play at Muscial to-night.  <br />Called for <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/communitymembers/#DwyerBeatrice" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Beatrice</a> &amp; then Beatrice,  <br />mamma &amp; I went up Wellington  <br />St. to meet <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/communitymembers/#McEdwardEthel" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Ethel McEdward</a>. When  <br />we got the programmes there was  <br />a mistake in printing &amp; my name  <br />was not on but I played just  <br />the same. <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/music/#GodardB" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">&quot;Au Matin&quot; by Godard</a>. <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/communitymembers/#ChapmanGenevra" onmouseover="showPreview(event)"> Miss  <br />G. Champman</a> came home with us  <br /><a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/communitymembers/#RobertsonE" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Eva Robertson</a> played a pretty  <br /><a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/music/#GodardB" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">&quot;Gigue&quot; by Godard</a>.</p>
</div>

<div id="1900-01-12">
<h2><span>&nbsp;</span><span>&nbsp;</span><span>Friday 12</span><span>(12-353)</span><span>&nbsp;</span></h2>

<p>Went to <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/locations#Lees" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Lee&#39;s</a> then to school.  <br /><a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/communitymembers/#GardinerAnnie" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Annie Gardiner</a> intended stopping  <br />and going to the Art School in  <br />Buffalo but is not going. <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/communitymembers/#Nelson" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Miss Nelson</a> is there. <a>Annie</a> &amp; I  <br />went uptown. She went to  <br /><a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/locations#AmericanBazaarStore" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Bazaar</a> &amp; I went to <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/locations#McLarenAndCo" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">McLaren&#39;s</a>  <br />after some hairpins &amp; to <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/locations#library" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">library</a> &amp;  <br /><a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/locations#Lees" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Lloyd&#39;s</a> after the teapot. Mamma <br />went over to <a>Mrs Pays</a> in afternoon.  <br /><a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/communitymembers/#AllieAunt" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Aunt Allie</a> sent me a  <br />birthday present. A book &quot;<a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/literature#Kedar" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">In  <br />the Tents of Kedar</a>. She has  <br />been in Buffalo &amp; just got home  <br />on the 9<sup style="font-size: x-small;">th</sup></p>
