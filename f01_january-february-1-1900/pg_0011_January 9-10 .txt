<h2><span>January</span><span>&nbsp;</span><span>Tuesday 9</span><span>(9-356)</span><span>1900</span></h2>

<p>Practised in the morning as I have<br />
to go to <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/locations/#CollegiateInstitute" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">school</a> this afternoon.<br />
Went to school and <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/communitymembers/#GardinerAnnie" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Annie Gardiner</a><br />
&amp; I sat together. Came home with<br />
the trio <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/communitymembers/#DwyerLeta" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Leta</a>, <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/communitymembers/#NewmanLaura" onmouseover="showPreview(event)"> Laura Newman</a> &amp; <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/communitymembers/#LloydNellie" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Nellie<br />
Lloyd</a>. I went with <a>Nell</a> around Duke<br />
St. Toronto is getting to be a bad<br />
place. A Miss Ferguerson was<br />
sandbagged on Friday night &amp; died,<br />
then last night a young boy<br />
was knocked down.</p>

<h2><span>&nbsp;</span><span>&nbsp;</span><span>Wednesday 10</span><span>(10-355)</span><span>&nbsp;</span></h2>

<p>Did not go to school to-day<br />
Mamma has been lengthening<br />
my Heather mixture skirt &amp; I wore<br />
it to-day. Went to Dwyer&#39;s after<br />
<a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/communitymembers/#DwyerBeatrice" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Beatrice</a> to go with me to try<br />
the piano in Wherry&#39;s Hall, but<br />
she &amp; <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/communitymembers/#HendersonMay" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">May Henderson</a> went out to<br />
<a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/communitymembers/#BarnesNellie" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Nellie Barnes</a> as she has been sick.<br />
Went &amp; got <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/communitymembers/#Nettie" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Nettie</a> to go with<br />
me. The one piano is a good one<br />
They are both <a href="http://www.pianotech.ca/nordheimer-pianos.htm">Nordheimers</a>. Papa<br />
&amp; mamma went to <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/communitymembers/#CoyMr" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Coy&#39;s</a><br />
at night. Papa got a nice<br />
calendar from the <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/locations#PetersCartridgeCo" onmouseover="showPreview(event)"> Peters<br />
Cartridge Co.</a> &amp; to-day at noon<br />
<a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/communitymembers/#WalkerJames" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">J.N . Walker</a> the popular druggist was<br />
married to-day to Miss Lake</p>
