<div id="1900-01-05">
<h2><span>January</span><span>&nbsp;</span><span>Friday 5</span><span>(5-360)</span><span>1900</span></h2>

<p>Sweep day. I read &quot;<a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/literature#Camerons" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">At the Camerons</a>&quot;<br />
in the &quot;<a>Harper&#39;s Young People</a>&quot; when<br />
mamma was sweeping. We had<br />
a beggar in afternoon asking for a<br />
few cents as he had a long way<br />
to go. <u>Rats</u>! Went over to <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/locations#Lees" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Lee&#39;s</a><br />
in afternoon after eggs. Took a<br />
&quot;Young Ladies Journal&quot; Mrs Pay lent<br />
me. Her kitty has a sore<br />
tail. Mrs Malcolmson came after<br />
eggs when I was there. She got<br />
large sum of 2. Had some candy<br />
at night.</p>
</div>

<div id="1900-01-06">
<h2><span>&nbsp;</span><span>&nbsp;</span><span>Saturday 6</span><span>(6-359)</span><span>&nbsp;</span></h2>

<p>Took my music lesson. <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/communitymembers#ReadAngelo" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Mr Read</a><br />
had not had his breakfast when<br />
I got there so <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/communitymembers#CampbellMrs" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Mrs Campbell</a><br />
showed me some of <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/communitymembers#CampbellRobert" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Robert<br />
Campbell&#39;s</a> sketches. Mr Read<br />
just had 2 new songs he<br />
designed the cover himself for<br />
them. Took the <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/music#CzernyCarl" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">6th Etude of<br />
Czerny Op 740</a>. Went to <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/locations#Eckhardts" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Eckhardt&#39;s</a> &amp;<br />
got my <a>Mathew&#39;s History of Music</a><br />
&amp; a scribbling pad. <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/locations#TrustySusan" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Mrs Trusty</a><br />
was here ironing. Mamma &amp; I<br />
went uptown in the afternoon.<br />
She got a very pretty chrysanthemum<br />
centerpiece at <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/locations#MissesAnderson" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Miss Anderson&#39;s</a> Papa got<br />
5 rabbits. Have Deceptive Cadences<br />
in Harmony for next week.</p>
