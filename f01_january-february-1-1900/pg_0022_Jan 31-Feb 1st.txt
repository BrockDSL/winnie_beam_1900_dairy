<h2><span>January</span><span>&nbsp;</span><span>Wednesday 31</span><span>(31-334)</span><span>1900</span></h2>

<p><a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/communitymembers/#TrustySusan" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Mrs Trusty</a> is here. Did not go to<br />
<a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/locations/#CollegiateInstitute" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">school</a> but went to <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/locations#library" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">library</a><br />
and got a book <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/literature/#SwanAnnie" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">&quot;The Guinea<br />
Stamp&quot; by Annie S Swan</a>.<br />
Mamma went to <a href="/s/Diary-of-Winnie-Beam/page/locations/#StThomas" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">church</a>, and<br />
they got $27. The Ladies Aid is doing<br />
all right. Mamma went to a<br />
musicale held at <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/communitymembers/#Larkin" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Mrs Larkin&#39;s</a><br />
for Red Cross fund. <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/communitymembers#CarmanWinnie" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Winnie</a><br />
and <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/communitymembers/#CoyMattie" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Mattie</a> sang, <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/communitymembers/#GrovesLouisaonmouseover=" showpreview="">Louisa Groves</a><br />
&amp; <a href="/s/Diary-of-Winnie-Beam/page/communitymembers/#Marquismaud" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Mrs Marquis</a> played and <a>Mr<br />
Garry</a>. Papa went to <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/locations#Masonic" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">lodge</a> and<br />
the <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/locations#CitizensHouse" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Citizens House</a> supper.</p>
</div>

<div id="1900-02-01">
<h2><span>February</span><span>&nbsp;</span><span>Thursday 1</span><span>(32-333)</span><span>&nbsp;</span></h2>

<p>Went to school in afternoon<br />
<a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/communitymembers/#GardinerAnnie" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Annie Gardiner</a> was there. She<br />
has not been there for a long<br />
while, She came to Queen street<br />
with me. I finished &quot;<a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/literature/#SwanAnnie" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">The Guinea<br />
Stamp</a>&quot; last night I am a very<br />
swift reader. Mamma went<br />
out to call on <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/communitymembers/#KlotzMarie" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Mrs Klotz</a>. She<br />
went uptown and bought me<br />
a pair of gray kid gloves<br />
for to-morrow afternoon.<br />
Did my German for to-morrow<br />
at night. Have 30 and 31 pages of<br />
&quot;<a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/literature/#Hauff" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Das Kalte Herz</a>&quot;.</p>
