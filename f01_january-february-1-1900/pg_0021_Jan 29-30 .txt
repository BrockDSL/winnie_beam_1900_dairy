<h2><span>January</span><span>&nbsp;</span><span>Monday 29</span><span>(29-336)</span><span>1900</span></h2>

<p>Went to <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/locations/#CollegiateInstitute" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">school</a> in the morning<br />
but <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/communitymembers#CloneyMiss" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Miss Cloney</a> had went<br />
home. I staid [sic] the hour and<br />
the girls had a high old time<br />
Called in at <a>Mrs Pay&#39;s</a> when I<br />
went as she wanted to know<br />
<a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/communitymembers#Notman" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Mrs Notman&#39;s</a> day. Mamma is<br />
working a chrysanthemum<br />
centerpiece. <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/communitymembers#BuntingMr" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Mr Bunting</a> came<br />
home with papa for tea<br />
and then they went to<br />
the <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/locations#GrandOperaHouse" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Opera House</a> to &#39;<a href="https://www.researchgate.net/publication/236716799_Forgotten_Manuscripts_A_Trip_to_Coontow">A Trip to<br />
Coontown</a>.&quot;</p>

<div id="1900-01-30">
<h2><span>&nbsp;</span><span>&nbsp;</span><span>Tuesday 30</span><span>(30-335)</span><span>&nbsp;</span></h2>

<p>They had examination at<br />
school so I did not have<br />
to go. <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/communitymembers#KillmerMrs" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Mrs Killmer</a> called on<br />
mamma in afternoon and<br />
brought back two books papa<br />
lent them. I have been<br />
working some on a doily.<br />
<a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/literature/#ParsonL" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Jerusha&#39;s Dow&#39;s Album</a> was<br />
repeated last night in <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/locations#StBarnabasChurch" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">St.<br />
Barnabas Church</a>. I got an<br />
invitation at noon <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/communitymembers/#CoyAllie" onmouseover="showPreview(event)"> Allie Coy</a><br />
is going to have an &quot;At Home&quot;<br />
Friday afternon from 4 to 6,<br />
Papa brought some buttercups<br />
and peppermints. <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/music#DePachman" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">DePachman</a><br />
the Russian painist played<br />
in Toronto last night.</p>
