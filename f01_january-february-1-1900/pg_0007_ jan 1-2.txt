<h2><span>January</span><span>New Year&#39;s Day (Dom.)</span><span>Monday 1</span><span>(1-364)</span><span>1900</span></h2>

<p>New Year. First day of 1900 <a href="/s/Diary-of-Winnie-Beam/page/communitymembers#MerrittCharlie" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Charlie<br />
Merritt</a> died at Grand Forks<br />
British Columbia yesterday of<br />
typhoid fever. To-day is<br />
election day and papa went<br />
up about 3 o&#39;clock and did<br />
not get back until nearly<br />
10. He brought us some candy<br />
chocolate drops. We had a very<br />
fat goose for dinner, not a<br />
very young one. Had to have<br />
good teeth.</p>
</div>

<div id="1900-01-02">
<h2><span>&nbsp;</span><span>&nbsp;</span><span>Tuesday 2</span><span>(2-363)</span><span>&nbsp;</span></h2>

<p>Went uptown in afternoon.<br />
Was up to <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/locations#Eckhardts" onmouseover="showPreview(event)"> Eckhardt&#39;s</a> but Miss<br />
Macfarlane was not there so I did<br />
not get what I wanted. Called at<br />
<a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/locations#DrBeam" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">office</a> and <a href="/s/Diary-of-Winnie-Beam/page/communitymembers#Nettie" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Nettie</a> came home<br />
with me for tea. <a href="/s/Diary-of-Winnie-Beam/page/communitymembers#CarmanMr" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Mr Carman</a><br />
came over and borrowed<br />
my <a href="/s/Diary-of-Winnie-Beam/page/literature#MildredsBooks" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Mildred books</a>. Then<br />
<a href="/s/Diary-of-Winnie-Beam/page/communitymembers#DudleyLeta" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Leta</a> came &amp; borrowed my<br />
German Grammar as she forgot<br />
hers &amp; left it at school before holiday.<br />
Papa, mamma, Nettie &amp; I played<br />
crokinole. Mamma &amp; I beat. The<br />
2<small><sup>nd</sup></small> Contingent left for Toronto today<br />
to go to South Africa. <a href="/s/Diary-of-Winnie-Beam/page/communitymembers#KingDr" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Dr King</a>&#39;s son<br />
went.</p>
