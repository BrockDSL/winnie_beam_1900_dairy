<h2><span>January</span><span>&nbsp;</span><span>Monday 15</span><span>(15-350)</span><span>1900</span></h2>

<p>Went to <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/locations/#CollegiateInstitute" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">school</a> in morning,<br />
but had my walk for<br />
nothing for they had the<br />
weekly examinations. <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/communitymembers/#Fowler" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Miss<br />
Fowler</a> on Queen St. died<br />
yesterday in the hospital.<br />
Papa got five books at<br />
night. Premaums [?] with the<br />
&quot;Hall Hour&quot; Four by <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/literature#DoyleArthur" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">A. Conan<br />
Doyle</a> and one by <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/literature#StevensonRobert" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Robert Louis<br />
Stevenson</a>. It rained very<br />
hard at night.</p>
</div>

<div id="1900-01-16">
<h2><span>&nbsp;</span><span>&nbsp;</span><span>Tuesday 16</span><span>(16-349)</span><span>&nbsp;</span></h2>

<p>Went to school in afternoon.<br />
Papa went to Hamilton to-night<br />
to the <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/locations#HamiltonGunClub" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Hamilton Gun Club</a> shoot.<br />
Called in <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/communitymembers/#DwyerAnna" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Dwyer&#39;s</a> in afternoon to<br />
see if she would go with<br />
mamma &amp; I to <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/locations#KnoxChurch" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Knox Church</a><br />
to-night. Mamma &amp; I called<br />
for her at night. They had an<br />
entertainment called &quot;<a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/literature#ParsonL" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Jerusha<br />
Dow&#39;s Family Album</a>. It<br />
was fine. <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/communitymembers/#McCliveBella" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Mrs McClive</a> was<br />
the grandmother and explained<br />
each picture. Mrs McGhie<br />
came home with us. Lost<br />
my little black pin from my<br />
skirt.</p>
</div>
