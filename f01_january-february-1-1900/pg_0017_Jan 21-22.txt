<h2><span>January</span><span>&nbsp;</span><span>Sunday 21</span><span>(21-344)</span><span>1900</span></h2>

<p>Went to <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/locations/#StThomas" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">church</a> in morning.&nbsp;<br />
Sermon was Phil. 3 &amp; the 1<small><sup>st</sup></small> &amp; second&nbsp;<br />
verses. <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/communitymembers/#MerrittMrs" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Mrs Louis Meritt</a> sat in&nbsp;<br />
our seat as theirs was filled.&nbsp;<br />
I finished the &quot;<a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/literature/#AllenJames" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Choir Invisible</a>&quot;<br />
this afternoon before Sunday<br />
School. Went to Sunday School&nbsp;<br />
<a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/communitymembers/#Haun" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Mrs Haun</a> gave me a &quot;Golden Text&nbsp;<br />
Book&quot; for year 1900. Came home&nbsp;<br />
with <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/communitymembers/#PriorMiss" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Lizzie</a>. <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/communitymembers/#Oloughlin" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Mr O&#39;Loughlins&nbsp;</a><br />
mother is not expected to live.<br />
Did not go to church at&nbsp;<br />
night.</p>

<div id="1900-01-22">
<h2><span>&nbsp;</span><span>&nbsp;</span><span>Monday 22</span><span>(22-343)</span><span>&nbsp;</span></h2>

<p>Did not got to <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/locations/#CollegiateInstitute" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">school</a> in the morning&nbsp;<br />
as I took &quot;Celery King&quot;<small><sup>1</sup></small>. Mamma went&nbsp;<br />
out collecting for ladies aid in the&nbsp;<br />
afternoon, and uptown after some&nbsp;<br />
silk for doily of chrysanthemums. I<br />
went uptown after some birdseed&nbsp;<br />
and cotton. Went to the office <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/communitymembers/#BuntingMr" onmouseover="showPreview(event)"> Mr<br />
Bunting</a> and Mrs McGhie was in there&nbsp;<br />
Met Helen and she said she was&nbsp;<br />
coming over. The entertainmnet in&nbsp;<br />
<a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/locations/#KnoxChurch" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Knox Church</a> was repeated to-night.<br />
<a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/communitymembers/#OLoughlin" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Mr O&#39;Loughlin&#39;s</a> mother died&nbsp;<br />
yesterday.</p>
<p>------<br />
<small>1. The product is a laxative.</small>
