<h2 class="writein">W. M. Beam Jan 4<sup>th</sup> 1901</h2>

<p>Tuesday January 29<small><sup>th</sup></small> 1901<br />
clear&nbsp;&amp; cold out. <a href="/s/Diary-of-Winnie-Beam/page/music/#Verdi" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Verdi</a> is dead.<br />
Died last week on Friday or Sat.<br />
The <a href="http://bmd.stcatharines.library.on.ca/en/3502633/image/3525810">Musical Circle</a> lecture is<br />
postponed. I went up to<br />
the <a href="/s/Diary-of-Winnie-Beam/page/locations#library" onmouseover="showpreview(event)">library</a> in the afternoon.<br />
Got one of <a href="/s/Diary-of-Winnie-Beam/page/literature/#AldenIsabella" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Pansey&#39;s</a> books<br />
<a href="/s/Diary-of-Winnie-Beam/page/literature/#AldenIsabella" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Miss Priscilla Hunter</a>&quot;. Papa<br />
has the gripes<small><sup>1</sup></small> ate to much candy<br />
<a>Mrs Marquis</a> came &amp; told<br />
us the meeting was postponed<br />
until Feb 12<small><sup>th</sup></small></p>
</div>

<div id="1901-01-30">
<h2>&nbsp;</h2>

<p>Wednesday Jan 30<small><sup>th</sup></small> 1901.<br />
<a href="/s/Diary-of-Winnie-Beam/page/communitymembers#TrustySusan" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Mrs Trusty</a> is here to-day.<br />
Mamma had to go to the<br />
<a href="/s/Diary-of-Winnie-Beam/page/locations#StThomas" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">church</a> &amp; to <a href="/s/Diary-of-Winnie-Beam/page/communitymembers#Sullivan" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Mrs Sullivan&#39;s</a><br />
She got some very pretty<br />
samples of waist goods. I am<br />
going to get one with pieces like<br />
honiton braid running through<br />
it. <a href="/s/Diary-of-Winnie-Beam/page/communitymembers#CarmanElla" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Ella</a> was in. Poor <a href="/s/Diary-of-Winnie-Beam/page/communitymembers#TuckerThomas" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Tommy</a><br />
is very sick with pneumonia.<br />
They have a debate to-night<br />
in the church between a<br />
boy &amp; girl. Last night our<br />
Society debated against Y.M.C.A<br />
in their rooms.<br />
<br />
------<br />
<small>1. Gastric or intestinal pain.</small></p>
