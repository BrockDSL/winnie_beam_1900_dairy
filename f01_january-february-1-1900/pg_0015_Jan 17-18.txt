<h2><span>&nbsp;</span><span>&nbsp;</span><span>Thursday 18</span><span>(18-347)</span><span>&nbsp;</span></h2>

<p>Practiced in the morning. Got<br />
ready to go to school, but it<br />
was raining. Waited for a<br />
while then went uptown.<br />
Got some Glycerine &amp; Celery King<br />
at <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/locations#WalkerAndAbbs" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Walker&#39;s &amp; Abbs</a>. Then to the<br />
<a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/locations#Lees" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">library</a>. Got <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/literature#AllenJames" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">&quot;The Choir Invisible&quot;,<br />
by James Lane Allen</a>. Went to<br />
<a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/locations#Eckhardts" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Eckhardt&#39;s</a> &amp; paid for my musical<br />
history. Got the papers. <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/communitymembers#BuntingMr" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Mr<br />
Bunting</a> was in the office<br />
Mamma went to <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/communitymembers/#Notman" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Mrs Notman&#39;s</a><br />
at Home. Mrs Pay and <a href="/s/ital-iasc-2q90/page/communitymembers/#Dudley" onmouseover="showPreview(event)">Mrs<br />
Dudley</a> were with her.</p>
